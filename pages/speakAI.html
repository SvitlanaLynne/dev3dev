I invite you to join my online learning sessions where we explore and speak AI together.

Format (50 min Online Meeting):

  • Research quietly (30 min): Everyone explores any aspect of AI individually — come up with your own topic.
  • Share findings (20 min): Everyone briefly shares just 1 surprising fact or 1 real-life use, speaking for only 3 minutes.

What to expect:

  • A shared learning space — not a networking event — about learning side by side.
  • A drop-in session — not a formal club — with no membership or booking required. Like a library, you can come and go freely.
  • A peer-to-peer exchange — not a lecture — where I'm learning and speaking alongside you so we can explore AI more effectively and enjoyably together.

    • Think of it as a group run on Halifax boardwalk...

Why join:

  • Make time to explore AI — tools, news, basics, history, or hands-on practice building Agents. This session reserves that time for your self-development each week.
  • Practice speaking about AI through short retelling. While solo or 'anytime' online learning remains primary, here we focus on impromptu speaking and real-world thinking on our feet.
  • Listen to others — you may be surprised by how differently AI can be used or perceived.
  • Share ideas — including practical tools for productivity, business, investment, or self-development.
  • Practice researching and presenting under time limits — a valuable skill in itself.
  • Apply discoveries directly to real-life tasks and projects.

I'm exploring AI myself and felt the experience could be richer if we practiced together. Let's speak AI!
