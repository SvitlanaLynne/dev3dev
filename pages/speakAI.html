I invite you to join my online learning sessions where we explore and speak AI together.

Format (50 min Online Meeting):

  • Research quietly (30 min): Everyone explores any aspect of AI individually — come up with your own topic.
  • Share findings (20 min): Everyone briefly (3 min) shares just 1 surprising fact or 1 real-life use.

What to expect:

  • A shared learning space — not a networking event.
  • A drop-in session — no membership or booking required.
  • A peer-to-peer exchange — not a lecture — where we learn together.

Think of it as a group run on Halifax boardwalk...

Why Join

  • Make time to explore AI tools, news, basics, history, or practice.
  • Practice impromptu speaking about AI.
  • Listen to diverse perspectives.
  • Share practical tools and ideas.
  • Practice presenting under time limits.
  • Apply discoveries to real-life projects.

I'm exploring AI myself and felt the experience could be richer if we practiced together.

Let's speak AI!
